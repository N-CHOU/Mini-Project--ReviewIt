"Mulan"

I feel like this was one of Disney's BEST movies to date! Let's not forget that I grew up watching the Cartoon/Animated version of this so I felt there was a LOT to follow up to. In this revised version Not only showed Women Empowerment and Family Devotion but it also showed that if you persevere and believe in yourself that you are capable of anything.

Nabodita Choudhury
"Run"

It's a very good movie, yet almost predictable.The acting quality of the actresses was off the charts, but Sarah Paulson maybe starts a little too much to appear in “ darker plots” and that might eventually label her. I say that because she is also very good in comedy and use that side too.Run is well idealized, then again predictable but what did let me down was the final minutes of the movie, when the same old “hurt people hurt people” comes back to haunt us all.

Arpita Saxena
"Parasite"

Parasite really is the kind of remarkable experience that makes modern movie-going/Binge-watching such a joy. South Korean filmmaker Bong Joon-ho already has an impeccable track record, but he's stepped up his game with this brilliant, powerfully revealing social satire.Filming a movie is like painting a picture, you need to put time and effort into it and boy the people who worked on this film knew what there doing.
